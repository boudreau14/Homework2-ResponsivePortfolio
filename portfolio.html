Beer Here - Brewery Finder

Beer Here is a site created for users to find their post-quarantine watering hole, or micro-watering hole, and also see when their favorite teams are playing that same day. This app shows the users no only what breweries are in your U.S. city of choice, but also where they are on a map down to their exact longitude and latitude.

Eat-Da-Burger!

BurgerTime (Eat-Da-Burger!) is a burger logger hosted via Heroku, that uses MySQL, Node, Express, Handlebars, with an ORM. It follows the MVC design pattern and uses Node and MySQL to query and route data, while Handlebars is used to generate the HTML.
