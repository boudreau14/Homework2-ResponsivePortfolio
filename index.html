About Me

Hi, I'm Bryan, a newly minted full-stack developer. I currently have a working knowledge of HTML, CSS, and basic Javascript, but have been promised that we'll soon be covering exciting topics such as Mongo DB, Express JS, React JS/Redux, Node JS, and more! I can't wait to update you on my progress.


For the past six years I've worked at McMaster-Carr, an industrial supply distributor, in many capacities. I began as an Account Manager, but quickly transitioned into an Information Technology Supervisor role, which reignited my childhood interest in web development. In 2015 I was moved into a Customer Service Manager position, which allowed me to focus on my communication skills, as well as lead a team od 30 in providing timely responses to our customers. Then, in 2017 I got the opportunity to progress into an Operations Management role, which has provided me with more insight into the company's processes and systems.
